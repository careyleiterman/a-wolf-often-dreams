About Me

My name is Carey and I struggled with anxiety, OCD and some other mental illnesses. Despite this, my life is filled with many other things. I work full time in a school and help children, who like myself have their own challenges in their everyday lives. I enjoy a variety of interests such as reading and writing. I also have pets and LOVE astronomy. So to get to know me a bit and understand why I created this website, please read on below.

Why Did I Create This Website?

I suppose you are wondering why I called this site, 'Scared of a Cockroach'. Well for starters I am scared of them and they really give me anxiety. However, that is not the sole reason for the name. I feel that in a website like this, there needs to be meaning behind it for people to be able to relate to it. For me, roaches represent everything that this website represents. They are something many people look at with disgust, fear, anxiety and panic; all things this website is about. Therefore I felt it was a good name for the site. There is a much more in depth story about my fear of roaches which you can read here.

My Struggles

I have long struggled with various forms of anxiety, OCD, and a variety of other mental illnesses. Throughout my life, starting at the age of six years old I have struggled to fit in, be comfortable with myself, and just be what everyone would classify as "normal". I really didn't develop most of my symptoms until I was in my middle teen years, and most of them have lasted into adulthood. Despite this I do have a relatively normal life and enjoy doing things just like anyone else. It is tough sometimes but I do the best I can and for the most part have a good life.

My Job

During the week I have a full time job where I work with special needs children, a stressful yet rewarding job that I love doing. Every day is a new experience and I look forward to making a difference every times I go to work. Some days are more better than others, and some are downright stressful. The children I work with range from emotionally disturbed to autistic. Each has their own needs and through helping them, it also helps me. By working with these kids, I realize that everyone has their own struggles, so it gives me hope that I can win the fight against mine. Overall it is a rewarding job and I look forward to making a difference in each life I come across.

My Interests

Like most of you, I have a variety of interests. First off, I love to read, especially the fantasy genre. At any given time you can catch me sitting back and enjoying a Mistborn novel by Brandon Sanderson or Wheel of Time series by the late Robert Jordan.

Besides reading, I also enjoy writing. I am currently working on two pieces. One is a fantasy novel with a really cool magic system. It is the typical sword and sorcery, epic fantasy novel that I hope to one day complete (if not publish) I am also working on a personal memoir which is part of what gave me the idea for this website. I've written many stories which I will include here as I continue my quest to write my life story.

I also enjoy drawing and sometimes painting, though I am not so good with the brush as I am with a pencil! I enjoy drawing landscapes and am working on faces right now. I am starting to get really good at drawing eyes and I will post some of my work in the near future for anyone who is interested to enjoy.

I spend a lot of time watching tv and movies. I have a wide variety of tastes and a large digital and blu-ray collection. Some of my favorites are Shawshank Redemption, The Italian Job, and Die Hard. I also love almost all of the MCU movies. On television I love the The Big Bang Theory and watch it pretty much every night.

When I am not home I enjoy hiking, working out at the gym, and visiting different cultural areas of New York City. Anything from a museum to the streets of Times Square is fair game. Sometimes I will even head out to a Yankees game to root for them as they go for another title.

Conclusion

As you can probably tell from above, just like everyone else, I have days that are relatively good where I can enjoy myself and have fun. However, there are times where things don't feel so great and even days where I feel the world is going to end. In other words: Life can be a struggle!

That is why I started this website. My thoughts are that if I struggle on a weekly, daily, or even hourly basis; so might some of you. It is my hope that through this site, I can bring together others who also struggle and through new friendships we might build ourselves stronger (and less stressful) lives. Even if I can help just one person though my efforts it will be a success. So if you're willing, come share your struggles with myself and anyone else who decides they want a change.
